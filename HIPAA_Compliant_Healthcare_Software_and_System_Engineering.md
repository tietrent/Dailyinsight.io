Core Components of Healthcare Software Development 

HIPAA Compliant Engineering

Security and privacy are essential in healthcare. Software must meet HIPAA, GDPR and SOC2 standards to protect electronic protected health information. This protects patients and reduces compliance risk for providers.


Electronic Health Record Integration and Interoperability

True interoperability means data flows reliably across systems using standards such as FHIR and HL7. Proper integration improves care coordination and eliminates redundant documentation.
